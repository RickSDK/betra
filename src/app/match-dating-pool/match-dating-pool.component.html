Intimacy Levels

Intimacy Level is the small number listed next to the person's name. It indicates how many Betra features the two people have used together. It is simply a small indicator, letting everyone know how serious the relationship might be getting.

Here are what the levels mean:

  1. One person is interested
  2. Match made
  3. Initial question asked
  4. Question replied to
  5. Open text exchange mode
  6. Contact Info exchanged
  7. Private picture exchanged
  8. Went on date
  9. Review written
