Become our first ever Bachelor/Bachelorette!

Here's how it works: Anyone can sign up to join the contest. On May 31st we will pick one lucky Bachelor and one lucky Bachelorette to compete in the contest.

We will also pick an additional 12 bachelors and 12 bacheloretts to fill their dating pools.

The 2 main contestants will then eliminate 2 people from their dating pools each week until the reach the final winner.

All members of our community will get to watch this play out in real time, and the contestants will fill out a weekly blog to describe their reasons for who they kept and eliminated. This will be a lot of fun for everyone!

For this first contest, it will be nation-wide and instead of going on real dates, they will simply have virtual dates. In the future, we hope to pick one city and do the contest on a more local level.

Good luck to everyone!
